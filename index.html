Classic Profile

Make a statement with strong, bold lines. The Classic profile offers a contemporary look with exceptional durability, perfect for modern Nigerian architecture.

Engineered specifically for tropical climates, our Classic profile provides superior weather resistance against heavy rains, intense UV rays, and strong winds common in Nigeria.

  • 50-year material warranty
  • Class 4 hail resistance
  • Fire resistant rating
  • Lightweight steel construction
  • Multiple color options

Bond Profile

Smooth flowing curves for a timeless appeal. The Bond profile combines elegance with functionality, ideal for both residential and commercial applications.

This profile offers excellent water shedding capabilities and wind resistance, making it perfect for Nigeria's rainy season and coastal areas.

  • Superior water drainage design
  • Wind resistance up to 120 mph
  • Stone coated finish
  • Corrosion resistant
  • Easy installation system

Shake Profile

The rustic charm of traditional shake with modern durability. Perfect for creating a natural, textured appearance that complements Nigeria's architectural heritage.

Combines the aesthetic appeal of wood shake with the strength and longevity of steel, offering the best of both worlds for discerning homeowners.

  • Authentic wood shake appearance
  • No maintenance required
  • Termite and pest resistant
  • Fade resistant colors
  • Environmentally friendly

Roman Profile

Distinctive elegance of the Mediterranean brought to Nigeria. The Roman profile offers a sophisticated tile appearance with superior performance.

Ideal for luxury homes and commercial buildings, providing the classic beauty of clay tiles without the weight and maintenance concerns.

  • Mediterranean tile appearance
  • Lightweight compared to clay tiles
  • Excellent thermal performance
  • Impact resistant
  • Long-lasting color retention

Tudor Profile

The elegance and style of Tudor architecture adapted for modern Nigerian homes. This profile offers a distinctive, premium appearance.

Perfect for creating architectural interest and curb appeal while providing exceptional protection against Nigeria's weather conditions.

  • Distinctive Tudor styling
  • Premium appearance
  • Excellent weather protection
  • Durable stone coating
  • Professional installation
